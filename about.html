Who We Are

Building Tools That Empower Everyone

Easy Web Tools is an India-based online platform offering a growing suite of free web-based tools designed to simplify your everyday digital tasks. From editing PDFs and generating QR codes to creating professional invoices — we provide everything you need, right in your browser.

We started with a simple belief: powerful tools shouldn't cost a thing. Too many online services lock essential features behind paywalls, subscriptions, or forced signups. We wanted to change that. Every tool we build is 100% free, requires no account creation, and processes your files securely in your own browser — your data never touches our servers.

Whether you're a student, freelancer, small business owner, or enterprise professional — Easy Web Tools is built for you.
