About QR Code Generator

What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) Code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information such as URLs, text, contact details, WiFi credentials, and more. When scanned with a smartphone camera or QR scanner app, the encoded information is instantly decoded and the appropriate action is triggered - opening a website, connecting to WiFi, saving a contact, or displaying text.

Why Use QR Codes?

QR codes have become essential in today's digital world. They bridge the gap between offline and online experiences, making it easy to share information without typing. Businesses use QR codes on business cards, product packaging, restaurant menus, marketing materials, and event tickets. Individuals use them to share WiFi passwords, contact information, and social media profiles.
